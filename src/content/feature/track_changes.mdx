# What is track changes?
Epsilon captures and records essential details about your data, including its origin, access history, and lineage. It tracks changes in permissions over time and maps connections between datasets and relevant metadata. All this information is easily accessible and auditable through intuitive, interactive tools, ensuring full transparency and control.
